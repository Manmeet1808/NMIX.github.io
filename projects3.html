  • This is the final website that I and a few other teammates created called OiStir. The purpose of this site is to provide a platform for users, specifically content creaters, screenwriters, and others within that type of field, to have a place to kickstart their creative process. OiStir focuses mainly on writers who want to write a script regarding the climate crisis. A link to the website can be found by clicking the image on the left.

    Once in the site, the user will first see a collection of words moving across the screen vertically. The movement of the words was set this way so that our site could properly reflect what might be going on in someone's brain as they are brainstorming. As the words move, the user has the ability to click any of the words to bring up curated audio clips that reference the chosen word as the main topic. The user can then listen to the clips and jot down notes to begin brainstorming. There is also a pause button on the top right if the user wants to stop the movement of the words at any point. The audio clips themselves are stored in firebase and are linked to their specific word. In this way, OiStir aims to help users gain the pearl of inspiration they need to create change!
